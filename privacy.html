Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 March 2026 · The Wish Wall · thewishwall.com

We collect only what we need, keep it only as long as necessary, and never sell your data. Your email address is used only to notify you when your wish is granted.

1. Who we are

The Wish Wall is operated by Lyrīon Ltd (Company No. 16825317), registered in England and Wales. Contact: hello@thewishwall.com

2. What data we collect

  • Email address (when you pin a wish or contact us)
  • Wish text (anonymised, displayed publicly on the wall)
  • IP address and approximate geolocation (via ipapi.co, retained max 30 days)
  • Payment reference (processed entirely by Stripe — we never see card details)
  • Browser type and device (standard server logs, retained max 90 days)

3. Why we collect it

  • To notify you when your wish is granted (email)
  • To display your wish on the wall (wish text)
  • To detect your country for display purposes (geolocation)
  • To prevent fraud and spam (IP address)
  • To process payments (Stripe handles all payment data)

4. Legal basis (UK GDPR)

We process your data under: (a) Contract performance — to deliver the service you paid for; (b) Legitimate interests — fraud prevention and platform security; (c) Consent — for marketing emails (you can opt out at any time).

5. Who we share data with

  • Stripe Payments UK Ltd — payment processing
  • Google Firebase (Cloud europe-west2) — database and hosting
  • ipapi.co — geolocation lookup (your IP is queried, no data stored by us)
  • Resend — transactional email delivery

We do not sell your data. Ever.

6. Data retention

  • Wish content: kept for the lifetime of the platform or until you request deletion
  • Email addresses: until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • IP/geolocation logs: maximum 30 days
  • KYC/identity records (for grant recipients): 5 years per the Money Laundering Regulations 2017

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to: access, correct, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing of your personal data. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk. To exercise any right, email hello@thewishwall.com.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies only to remember your cookie preference. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be notified by email to registered wish-makers.
