⏸ Wish Submissions

Pause or resume new wish submissions on the main site. When paused, the post form is disabled and visitors see a message.

⚡ Flush Queue

Promote all wishes with status queued to approved in a single batch write. Useful after reviewing a batch of incoming wishes.

Trigger a Magic Pool balance update email to subscribers via Resend. Uses the current pot balance from the ledger.

📥 Export Wishes CSV

Download all wishes — including their status, country, date, and grant cost — as a CSV file for offline analysis.
