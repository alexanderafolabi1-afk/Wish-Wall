01 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit. We use localStorage (similar in function to cookies) as our primary storage mechanism for preferences and session data.

04 Managing Your Preferences

When you first visit The Wish Wall, a cookie consent banner will appear. You can choose to accept all cookies or essential cookies only. You can change your preference at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or contacting us.

To clear your preferences in most browsers: Settings → Privacy → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data.
