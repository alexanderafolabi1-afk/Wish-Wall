Security Policy 
Supported Versions 
We actively monitor and patch the following versions to ensure the integrity of our platform and user data. 
Version Supported 
5.1.x:white_check_mark: 5.0.x:x: 4.0.x:white_check_mark: < 4.0:x:
Reporting a VulnerabilityIf you discover a potential security vulnerability, please do not use the public issue tracker. To protect our community, we follow a responsible disclosure process.
How to Report
Please email your findings to admin@wallwishes.xyz. 
To help us address the issue quickly, please include:A detailed description of the vulnerability.
The specific version and environment where it was found.
Clear steps to reproduce the issue or a proof-of-concept.
What to Expect 
Acknowledgement: You will receive a response within 48 hours confirming we have received your report. 
Evaluation: Our team will investigate and provide a timeline for a potential fix within 5 business days. 
Communication: We will keep you updated throughout the patching process until the vulnerability is resolved. 
Recognition: With your permission, we will credit your contribution once the fix is publicly released.
