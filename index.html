How the Magic Works ✨

Whimsical? Absolutely. Real? 100%.

📌

Step 1 — Pin a Wish

Write your wish (max 200 characters), pay 2 WishCoins ($2 USD) via Stripe, and your wish appears on the wall instantly. Your real name is required for verification — but you choose whether it shows on the wall. Anonymous. No judgement. Open to everyone, everywhere. Up to 3 wishes per year.

🛡️

Step 2 — Guardians Appear

Community Guardians browse the wall and sponsor wishes they believe in. They can fund a specific wish or drop coins into the Magic Pool for any wish to draw from.

✅

Step 3 — Wishes Get Granted

When a wish is fulfilled, the note is ticked green ✅ with a smile on the wall. Cheeky requests get a playful response stamp. The wisher gets an email. We never reveal their identity.

🧱

The Wall as Mosaic

The 1,000-spot wall always stays full — 1,000 wishes, 1,000 stories. Together the notes form a mosaic image — a giant "Thank You ❤️" — with each note carrying one tile of that design. When granted wishes are replaced after 4 hours, new tiles join the mosaic. The wall is never empty. The message never changes.

📊

Radical Transparency

Every penny is accounted for in the Magic Ledger. The 2 WishCoin fee covers platform costs. Guardian donations go directly to granting wishes. We're a community project — not a corporation. Questions? Email [email protected]

How We Grant Wishes: When the Magic Pool covers a wish's estimated cost, our team verifies the request and contacts the recipient by email. Grants are sent directly via PayPal or bank transfer in the recipient's name. We retain a 5% platform administration fee on each granted wish to cover operating costs, which is disclosed in our Transparency Ledger. We do not guarantee that every wish will be granted — grants depend on available funds and wish verification. We reserve the right to decline or remove wishes that violate our community standards.
Disclaimer: The Wish Wall is a community kindness platform, not a registered charity. WishCoins are a platform unit of account — 1 WishCoin = $1 USD. Donations are voluntary. The Wish Wall was born in December 2024 — quietly, on a cold night, after watching someone in a supermarket queue put items back at the checkout because they didn't quite have enough.

We didn't do anything that night. But we couldn't stop thinking about it.

So we built this. No grand mission statement. No corporate backing. Just a simple question:
"What if strangers helped strangers? What if that was the whole idea?"

We're still asking it, every day. And every wish on that wall is the answer.

— The Wish Wall, December 2024
