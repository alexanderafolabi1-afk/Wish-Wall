02 Complaints

We take complaints seriously. If you are unhappy with any aspect of Wall Wishes, please email admin@wallwishes.xyz with the subject line "Complaint". We will acknowledge your complaint within 5 working days and provide a full written response within 30 days.

If your complaint relates to the handling of your personal data and you are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint or by phone: 0303 123 1113.

03 Press & Partnerships

For media enquiries, partnership proposals, or collaboration opportunities, please email admin@wallwishes.xyz with the subject line "Press & Partnerships".

04 Technical Issues

If you are experiencing a technical problem with the platform, such as a wish not loading, a payment error, or an issue with your WishCredits, please email admin@wallwishes.xyz with the subject line "Technical Issue" and a brief description of the problem.
